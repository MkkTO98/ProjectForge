# Folder Summary: artifacts/reports

Artifact role: durable governance history for reports.

## Purpose
Reports preserve review, audit, investigation, architecture-to-reality, implementation, or synthesis findings that need to remain inspectable after the session.

## Report record should answer
- What was reviewed or audited?
- What evidence was used?
- What findings were accepted, rejected, or deferred?
- What risks or follow-up decisions were identified?

## Rules
- Reports are evidence and findings, not automatic task creation.
- Architecture-to-reality audit reports belong here unless a project-specific decision chooses a narrower subfolder.
- Do not rewrite historical findings merely to clean up the narrative; append, supersede, or create a new report.
